## Budget Justification

### Why Zero Cost?

This is an **academic project** (CSE 400) developed as part of our Bachelor's degree requirements. All resources were obtained through:

1. **Free Tier Services**
   - MongoDB Atlas: 512MB free tier
   - Vercel: Unlimited personal projects
   - Cloudinary: 25GB free storage
   - Arcjet: Free tier for security

2. **Open Source Tools**
   - All development tools are free and open-source
   - VS Code, Git, Node.js, React, etc.

3. **Student Resources**
   - GitHub Student Developer Pack (free domain, tools)
   - Personal laptops (already owned)
   - University internet and facilities

4. **Volunteer Development**
   - 1,920 hours of development time contributed by team
   - No salary or compensation
